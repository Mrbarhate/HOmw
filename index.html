BLOW MOLDING : PLASTIC BOTTLE


This is one of my amazing experience working with ANSYS to analyze stresses generated in the glassy material in blow molding process. As considering the basic fact about glass that it will be brittle below transition temperature and it softens as temperature will increase beyond transition temperature. It will be more or less like a viscoelastic in this transition region. This can be analyzed very efficiently using Ansys Polyflow 15.0.

Package Used: ANSYS Polyflow 15.0, Material Science
